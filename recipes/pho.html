Pho Recipe

Description

Pho is a Vietnamese dish of slow-simmered broth, typically prepared with beef bones, served with rice noodles, protein (such as beef, chicken or plant-based protein) and toppings.

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Noodles
  • Water

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Make broth
  3. Strain broth
  4. Bring a pot of water to a boil.
  5. Serve and enjoy!
